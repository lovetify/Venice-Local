Help & Tips

  1. Create an account or sign in to save favorites and post reviews. Guests can browse only.
  2. Browse businesses, filter by category, sort by rating/reviews, and open Details to see full info.
  3. Tap the heart “Save” to favorite a business; it turns orange when saved.
  4. Owners can add or edit their own businesses from the Owner Dashboard.
  5. Use the deals pill to spot current specials. Add a clear storefront photo for best results.
